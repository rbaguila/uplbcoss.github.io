OBJECTIVES

  • To promote the application of Computer Science and its related fields to socially relevant issues within the Philippine society.
  • To excel in every endeavor (not just in academics) and every challenge faced by every member.
  • To involve the members in matters concerning the University and the Filipino community.
  • To promote a sense of unity and brotherhood among the members of the organization through participation of its activities.
